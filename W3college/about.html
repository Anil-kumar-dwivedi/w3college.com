DOCUMENTATION

w3college is an online learning platform which aims to provide students, the at most guidance in the domains of Computer Science, Data Structures and Algorithms, Programming languages, Android/Web Development and Data Analytics. Its learning experience arranges coursework into a series of modules that include well-explained tutorials, examples, practice questions and a discussion forum to educate and assist students during the course of their degree as well as for placement preparations. The certificates issued on completion of courses will help candidates in their career advancement.



MODULE DESCRIPTION

The login page allows a user to enter username and password or authenticate using their google account. New users will be presented with a sign-up option to get themselves registered. On successful authentication, user gains access to the website and is directed to the main page. Otherwise, an error message about the login failure shows up and user can reset credentials with forgot password.

Comprise of easy-to-understand lessons in the various fields from the beginners to competitive level to master in their area of interests. This will help learners to gain complete knowledge and understanding of the subjects.
